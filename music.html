MUSIC

Music often goes along with yoga practices. It helps to reduce tension in the body and mind. You can listen to instruments, play them or move to the beat of the music. I like listening to them. I particularly enjoy singing bowls and wind chimes music. Both have a soothing musical sound that leads me to deep relaxation, stress relief and better sleep.
